Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Alpha Nex ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform for AI training data collection and review services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information
  • Account Information: Username, email address, password (hashed)
  • Profile Data: User role, XP points, account verification status
  • Communication Data: Messages, reviews, and descriptions you provide
2.2 Content Information
  • Uploaded Files: Videos, audio, documents, code, and associated metadata
  • File Metadata: File size, type, upload timestamp, description
  • Content Hashes: For duplicate detection and content integrity
2.3 Usage Information
  • Activity Logs: Upload history, review activity, login times
  • Performance Data: XP earned, content ratings, leaderboard positions
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information
2.4 Legal Consent Data
  • Consent Records: Timestamps, IP addresses, user agent strings
  • Agreement History: Terms acceptance, upload agreements, policy acknowledgments

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision
  • Creating and managing your account
  • Processing content uploads and reviews
  • Calculating and distributing XP rewards
  • Providing customer support
3.2 AI Training Purposes
  • Using uploaded content to train machine learning models
  • Improving AI systems and algorithms
  • Research and development activities
  • Creating training datasets for third parties (with your consent)
3.3 Platform Improvement
  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve our services
  • Detecting and preventing fraud or abuse
  • Maintaining platform security and integrity
  • Developing new features and functionality
3.4 Communication
  • Sending verification emails and notifications
  • Providing updates about your account or our services
  • Responding to your inquiries and requests
  • Sending important policy or terms updates

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 With Your Consent
  • Sharing content for AI training with third parties
  • Displaying your username on leaderboards
  • Sharing aggregated performance statistics
4.2 Service Providers
  • Cloud hosting and storage providers
  • Email service providers
  • Payment processing services
  • Analytics and monitoring services
4.3 Legal Requirements
  • Compliance with applicable laws and regulations
  • Response to legal process or government requests
  • Protection of our rights and property
  • Emergency situations involving safety
4.4 Business Transfers
  • In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales
  • Transfer to successor entities
  • Bankruptcy or similar proceedings

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Storage Locations
  • Data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure
  • Multiple geographic locations for redundancy
  • Compliance with regional data protection laws
5.2 Security Measures
  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

5.3 Data Retention
